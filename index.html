This is my personal website. Here I explore Creations—original essays and matrix readings exploring the structural models of ultimacy in philosophy and history—alongside Translations, where I bring primary texts from Arabic, Persian, Sanskrit, and English into the Bengali language. One is the pursuit of generating new structural frameworks, the other is the discipline of faithfully transmitting ancient ones.
