Khan Muhammad Bin Asad

This is my personal website. The website has three sections: Creations, Translations and Visions. Creations contain my original readings of humanity as a whole, based solely on its three main intellectual projects: science, philosophy and religion. Translations contain my translations of world literature into Bangla from various languages — mainly Persian, Latin, Sanskrit and English. Visions contain my readings of the artistic creations of humanity as a whole, across its three main forms: film, music and visual art. The site itself is brought into being through deep and active collaboration with various models of Claude and Gemini.
